## 🚀 Customer Intelligence & Revenue Optimization

### 📌 Objective
Analyse customer data to identify churn drivers, segment customers, and improve revenue strategies.

### 🛠 Tools Used
Python, Pandas, Scikit-learn, Power BI

### 📊 Key Insights
- High churn in month-to-month contracts
- Low tenure customers are at risk
- Top customers drive majority revenue

### 🤖 Model
Logistic Regression model to predict churn

### 💡 Business Recommendations
- Improve retention strategies
- Target high-value customers
- Optimize pricing
